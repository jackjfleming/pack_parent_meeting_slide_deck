Our Mission

Our pack is dedicated to the core mission of Cub Scouting: building character, learning skills, and having fun in a family-friendly environment.

Think of it as "Fun with a Purpose." We aim to help our Scouts:

  • Do their best and help other people.
  • Develop leadership skills and good citizenship.
  • Grow into their best selves, mentally and physically.

Understanding the Costs

Annual Dues

$85 - Scouting America National Fee

(Covers insurance, program development)

$50 - Pack 131 Program Fee

(Covers awards, Pinewood Derby kits, meeting supplies, and next year's neckerchief/slide)

Total: $135 / year

Activity Fees (Pay as you go)

~$30 / family - Fall & Spring Campouts

(Covers food and campsite costs)

~$20 / family - Blue & Gold Banquet

(Helps cover cost of our year-end celebration)

One-Time Uniform Cost (New Scouts)

This is a one-time startup cost. We recommend buying the shirt one size large to last multiple years.

  • Lion (K): ~$51
  • Tiger/Wolf/Bear (1-3): ~$86
  • Webelos/AOL (4-5): ~$108

Example Cost Summary

Fundraising: Fueling our Fun!

Our pack's only major fundraiser is our annual Popcorn Sale in October.

  • We partner with a local, family-owned company: Mom and Pop Popcorn.
  • Participation is highly encouraged, but there are no minimum sales requirements.
  • Funds raised are critical for keeping our program costs low and help pay for our Pinewood Derby track, event food, and supplies.
  • We offer great prizes for top sellers!

Volunteering

"Many hands make light work."

Our pack is run 100% by parent volunteers. To provide a great program and prevent burnout, we need every family to help in some capacity.

You don't need to be an expert! The only requirement is a willingness to have fun with the kids. Scouting America provides excellent online den leader guides that make planning simple and fast.
